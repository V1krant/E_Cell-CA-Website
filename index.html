About Us



Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kanpur, is a non-profit organization established to nurture entrepreneurial culture in IITK, and open doors for our next generation innovators, entrepreneurs and changemakers to get out there and leave their marks on the world. Owing to our well-established network, we work in collaboration with acclaimed Venture capitalists, PE Investors, Startup firms and Incubation centers, to provide every out of the box proposal the chance and the vital means and resources to one day become the next ‘Big Idea’.
The Campus Ambassador program is our effort to create an entrepreneurial web of college students across the country and provide them with ample opportunities to develop and revolutionize the start-up world. The selected Campus Ambassadors will work collectively with our team and become the face of E-Cell, IIT Kanpur in their respective campuses. The program will give them the room to enhance their skill-set while simultaneously bringing about entrepreneurial advancement in their colleges.

Why CA?



Acquire Skills

Working with one of the most dedicated teams from IITK will help you in acquiring more skills and professionalism. You will add many new dimensions to your personality along the way by enhancing your Communication, marketing, and management skills. Learn more about entrepreneurship and increase your contacts through various events at E-Cell.

Certificate of appreciation

Get certificate of appreciation from the E-Cell , IITK after the successful completion of tenure. This certificate along with being a highlight in your resume will also be a good source of motivation.

Organise events

Get the opportunity to organize various entrepreneurship-related events in your college in collaboration with E cell ITK. This will give you a great first-hand experience of event management and will also introduce you to the creative side of yours.

Get updated

Stay updated about the business world and develop a better understanding and possibilities of the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship. Get to know about the startups of IITK and acquaint yourself with the current vogue of entrepreneurship.
